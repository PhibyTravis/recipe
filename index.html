Chicken stew

This is a delicacy all across Kenya. Here 's how to prepare it

ingredients

  • kienyeji chicken
  • garlic
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • salt to test
  • cooking oil

procedure

  1. Boil the chicken with salt,garlic and ginger till tender
  2. In a pan add heat cooking oil till hot.
  3. Add your boiled chicken in buthes.Fry till brown
  4. In a sufuria fry onions till it start browning.
  5. Add in your chicken and stir.
  6. Add blended tomatoes and cover the sufuria.
  7. Cook under low heat till tomatoes are fully cooked
  8. Add little water.Let it simmer for some minutes.
  9. Ganish with dhania.
